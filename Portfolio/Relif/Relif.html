Relif

An application concept to help provide mental health to victims of unpredictable emergencies

My Role

I provided the team with most of the research dictating the direction of our project. I used my knowledge of finding information online to my advantage, and helped the team better understand where we could create the most value.

Project Overview

Relif is an app which connects natural disaster victims to volunteer psychologists. We found that currently there is no platform in existence which does this.

Research Methods

Our team used guerilla research techniques, like interviewing local residents and the police department, in order to get a general understanding of emergency response procedures. We then conducted large amounts of secondary research to narrow in on mental health for natural disaster survivors.

Going Forward

We would like to user-test our application on people who have been victims of natural disasters in the past to see what they would think. This would provide us with a proof of concept, and dictate our direction going forward with the project.
