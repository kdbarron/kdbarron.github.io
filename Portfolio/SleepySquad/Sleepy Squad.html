Sleepy Squad.

Helping children get back to sleep if they've woken up in the middle of the night

My Role

I acted as a lead game designer for this project. I made sure playtesting was done to test out our game's engagement, while providing critical insights in how to change our game.

Project Overview

Sleepy Squad is a game designed to help children go back to sleep at night if they have woken up. Our design constraint on the project was that it had to focus on devices. We created a game which tries to stop children from checking their phones at night by building a sleepy-squadmate.

Accomplishments

Philips Health, a company which took interest in our project, loved the idea of Sleepy-Squad. We were asked to present our game at the conference. We also presented our project at Philips Health in Pittsburgh.

Going Forward

We would have liked to user test our project on children who have problems falling back to sleep at night. Their insights would have been invaluable in our design of the sleepy-squadmates.
